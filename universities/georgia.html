Why Choose MBBS in Georgia?

  • World-class medical universities recognized by NMC, WHO, and other international bodies
  • No IELTS/TOEFL required for many universities
  • Modern infrastructure and research facilities
  • Student-friendly academic environment with 1:20 teacher-student ratio
  • 95% literacy rate reflecting strong academic excellence
  • English-medium programs eliminating language barriers

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 17 years old by December 31 of admission year
  • NEET qualification required
  • 50% in PCB (12th grade)
  • Valid Indian nationality certificate
  • No IELTS/TOEFL required for most universities

How to Apply

  1. Choose a university
  2. Check eligibility
  3. Submit application with required documents
  4. Appear for medical entrance test if required
  5. Attend personal interview
  6. Receive admission letter
  7. Apply for student visa
  8. Prepare for travel and accommodation
