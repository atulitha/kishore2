Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate (Bachelor's in Engineering)

  • Minimum 17 years old by start of course
  • 12th grade with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics (PCM), with high marks (75%+ recommended)
  • IELTS/TOEFL for English proficiency (IELTS 6.0–6.5)
  • UCAS application required

Postgraduate (Master's in Engineering/MSc)

  • Bachelor's degree in engineering or closely related field
  • Strong academic record (equivalent of UK 2:1 or 60–70%+)
  • IELTS/TOEFL (IELTS 6.5–7.0 or equivalent)
  • Relevant work or research experience (optional but beneficial)

How to Apply

  1. Research and shortlist universities & programs
  2. Check eligibility criteria and deadlines (September/January intake)
  3. Take IELTS/TOEFL if required
  4. Apply via UCAS (for UG) or directly to universities (for PG) with transcripts, SOP, LORs, resume, and passport copy
  5. Receive Conditional/Unconditional Offer
  6. Apply for UK Student Visa (Tier-4)
  7. Arrange travel, housing, and attend pre-departure orientation
