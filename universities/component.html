  1. Bachelor's in engineering or related field

How to Apply

For Engineering Programs

  1. Research and shortlist universities & programs
  2. Check eligibility and language requirements
  3. Take IELTS/TOEFL or TestDaF as needed
  4. Apply through Uni-Assist or directly to universities
  5. Receive admission letter
  6. Apply for German Student Visa
  7. Open blocked account (€11,208/year)
  8. Book travel and enroll

For Business Programs

  1. Shortlist programs (check DAAD.de for options)
  2. Take IELTS/TOEFL and GMAT (if required)
  3. Prepare application documents
  4. Apply through university portals
  5. Receive admission decision
  6. Apply for student visa and arrange finances
  7. Secure accommodation and health insurance
  8. Travel to Germany and enroll
