  • Basic Eligibility Requirements
    • Age Limit: Minimum 17 years by December 31 of the admission year, Maximum: 25 years
    • NEET Qualification: Mandatory as per NMC guidelines (no additional entrance exams required)
    • Academic Requirements: Minimum 50% aggregate in PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) in 12th standard, 40% for SC/ST/OBC categories
    • Nationality: Must possess a valid Nationality Certificate or Indian citizenship proof
  • Education Loans for Indian Students Pursuing MBBS Abroad
    • Loan Eligibility and Amount: Depends on academic credentials, course fees, and family financial profile.
    • Required Documents: NEET scorecard, university admission/invitation letter, detailed fee structure.
    • Financial Assessment: Parents’/guardians’ income, property, and assets are crucial for loan sanctioning.
  • Additional Notes
    • No entrance exams for MBBS in Russia except NEET.
    • Applicant should be 17 years by December 31 of the year of admission. Maximum age limit is 25 years.
    • Aggregate 50% in PCB for general candidates and 40% for reserved category students.
    • Applicants should submit their nationality certificate before applying for MBBS abroad.
