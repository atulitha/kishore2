Why Choose Russia for MBBS?

  • High-quality education, globally recognized degrees (WHO, NMC, ECFMG, FAIMER)
  • Affordable tuition and living costs
  • English-medium instruction
  • No donation/capitation fees
  • Strong clinical exposure and high FMGE pass rate

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 17 years old by December 31 of admission year
  • NEET qualification required
  • 50% in PCB (12th grade), 40% for reserved categories
  • Valid Indian nationality certificate

How to Apply

  1. Choose a university
  2. Check eligibility
  3. Submit application with required documents
  4. Receive admission letter
  5. Apply for student visa
  6. Book travel and attend pre-departure orientation
